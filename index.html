All About Moon Pages

Under the Moon Publishing Company that was founded on 2013 by the current Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Mr. Zarrick Amiel "Zamiel" Mercadejas. Moon Publishing Company is still known and on top for its best quality print that made the company win its awards as the Most Outstanding Publisher of Novels at the 2015 Philippine Business Achievers Awards and at the 2017 Philippine Marketing Excellence for Most Outstanding Book Manufacturer. These honor of recognition that the company had won won’t be possible without the continuous support of the public.

Serving for almost eight years, the Moon Publishing Company and Mr. Mercadejas promise to keep on producing quality and excellent products and are willing and prepared for more years of service and whatever the future may hold.

About the Founder

Mr. Mercadejas is the one behind the early success of the company despite being new in the industry. He continuously brings accomplishment to the company by building branches along the cities and malls that gives opportunities to people who need a job. The idea of making his own publishing company started when he was 16 years old where he began to love reading and collecting books. He was amazed at how the smell of the pages of a new book and the sight of shelves filled with books gives relaxation and sanity to him.

From that day, he wanted to share and spread the admiration he has for books so he started to build a publishing company that will print pages from the author and genre. He is overwhelmed on how the people loved and appreciated the quality they gave to the books people loved and waited to be published. He had a vision and a goal of bringing the best quality books every author and book lover deserved.

THE FAULT IN OUR STARS

Despite the tumor-shrinking medical miracle that has bought her a few years, Hazel has never been anything but terminal, her final chapter inscribed upon diagnosis. But when a gorgeous plot twist named Augustus Waters suddenly appears at Cancer Kid Support Group, Hazel’s story is about to be completely rewritten.

THE DA VINCI CODE

While in Paris, Harvard symbologist Robert Langdon is awakened by a phone call in the dead of the night. The elderly curator of the Louvre has been murdered inside the museum; his body covered in baffling symbols. As Langdon and gifted French cryptologist Sophie Neveu sort through the bizarre riddles, they are stunned to discover a trail of clues hidden in the works of Leonardo da Vinci—clues visible for all to see and yet ingeniously disguised by the painter...

Even more startling, the late curator was involved in the Priory of Sion—a secret society whose members included Sir Isaac Newton, Victor Hugo, and Da Vinci—and he guarded a breathtaking historical secret. Unless Langdon and Neveu can decipher the labyrinthine puzzle—while avoiding the faceless adversary who shadows their every move—the explosive, ancient truth will be lost forever.
